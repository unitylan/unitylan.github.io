The DeBeer Story

Our farm’s journey began over four generations ago with the DeBeer family’s dream to provide quality, locally-sourced meat. Starting with a few cattle, we’ve grown to raise goats, pigs, chickens, and other livestock. Throughout our growth, we've always remained committed to sustainability and ethical farming practices. We believe in farming as a way of life, where every decision we make reflects our deep respect for the land and our animals.

What sets us apart is not just the quality of our meat but our commitment to environmental stewardship. We treat our animals like family, providing them with stress-free, comfortable environments to thrive. By implementing sustainable grazing methods and focusing on long-term land preservation, we are creating a legacy of responsible farming that we can pass on for generations to come.

Our Environmental Commitment

At DeBeer Cattle Farm, sustainability isn’t just a buzzword—it’s the foundation of everything we do. We believe that responsible farming practices are essential not only for the land but also for the health of our animals and the community we serve. Our approach is holistic, focusing on long-term environmental stewardship while ensuring the highest quality, ethically-raised livestock.

We use rotational grazing to maintain healthy pastures and reduce the need for chemical fertilizers. By moving livestock across different sections of the farm, we allow the grass to regenerate and prevent overgrazing. This practice ensures that our animals always have access to fresh, nutrient-rich forage year-round.

Water conservation is another key priority for us. We’ve invested in efficient irrigation systems that reduce waste, ensuring we manage our water usage, particularly during the drier months. We also harvest rainwater, providing an additional water source that helps us minimize dependence on groundwater.

Our commitment extends to reducing our carbon footprint. We’ve implemented renewable energy solutions, including solar panels for electricity and biofuel systems for heating. We are also exploring alternative feed options that are both sustainable and cost-effective, further aligning our operations with our environmental goals.
