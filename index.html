Our Cattle Breeds

Afrikaner

Category: Beef Cattle

  • 2.5 years old
  • Muscular, strong frame, healthy udders
  • 613kg
  • Reddish

Afrikaner cattle are tough and resilient, perfect for South Africa's harsh, hot conditions. They’re low-maintenance, disease-resistant, and known for producing high-quality beef. Their muscular build and red to light brown coats make them an ideal choice for beef production in tough climates.

Status: For Sale

Brahman

Category: Beef & Dairy Cattle

  • 4 years old
  • Solid frame, distinct hump, healthy coat
  • 817.53kg
  • Light grey

Brahman cattle are heat-resistant and disease-tolerant, making them perfect for warmer regions. They grow large and produce excellent beef, thriving on pasture with minimal care. With their grey or red coats and muscular build, they are a top choice for beef farmers in hot climates.

Status: For Sale

Nguni

Category: Beef & Dairy Cattle

  • 3.1 years old
  • Compact, muscular, healthy coat, good hooves
  • 427kg
  • Dark brown

Nguni cattle are adaptable, disease-resistant, and thrive in diverse conditions. They are a versatile breed, excellent for both meat and milk production. Their beautiful variety of colours and patterns, along with their hardiness, make them ideal for small-scale, sustainable farming.

Status: For Sale

Simmental

Category: Beef Cattle

  • 2 years old
  • Large, well-muscled, strong frame
  • 789kg
  • Red and White

Simmental cattle are fast-growing and high-yielding, making them ideal for both beef and milk production. They are known for their yellow to reddish-brown coats and excellent feed conversion rates. Simmentals are perfect for commercial farms looking to maximize meat and dairy production.

Status: For Sale

Hereford

Category: Beef Cattle

  • 2.3 years old
  • Healthy and muscular, good fat cover
  • 850.2kg
  • Dark brown

Hereford cattle are calm, easy to handle, and produce top-quality beef with excellent marbling. They adapt well to various climates and are known for their consistent meat quality. Their red coat with a white face and great feed conversion make them a great choice for premium beef production.

Status: For Sale

The DeBeer Journey

  1. DeBeer Farm Founded

    Founded in 2004

    DeBeer Farm was founded with the goal of providing quality, ethically-raised produce while maintaining a sustainable approach.

  2. Expansion to Livestock Farming

    Expanded in 2008

    DeBeer Farm expanded to include free-range livestock, marking a significant milestone in supplying local markets with ethically-raised meat.

  3. Adoption of Sustainable Practices

    Sustainability adopted in 2015

    In 2015, DeBeer Farm fully transitioned to sustainable farming practices, enhancing soil health and protecting the environment.

  4. Expansion of Community Engagement

    Community Engagement in 2020

    DeBeer Farm significantly increased its community involvement, hosting farm tours and educational events.

  5. Continued Commitment to Growth

    In Progress - 2023

    Today, DeBeer Farm continues to grow, adapt, and innovate. Our commitment to sustainability, ethical farming practices, and community engagement drives our day-to-day operations, with plans to expand our services and offerings even further in the coming years.
